# Privacy Policy for Waffle Wednesday

**Last Updated: November 3, 2025**

## Introduction

Waffle Wednesday ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information You Provide

- **Account Information**: When you sign in with Apple, we collect your Apple ID and display name
- **Profile Information**: Profile pictures you choose to upload
- **Audio Recordings**: Voice recordings (waffles) you create and share through the app
- **Contact Information**: Friends list based on phone contacts you choose to share

### Automatically Collected Information

- **Usage Data**: Information about how you interact with the app
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version
- **Authentication Tokens**: Necessary for maintaining your login session

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

- Provide and maintain the Waffle Wednesday service
- Enable you to create and share audio waffles with friends
- Display your profile to other users
- Facilitate social interactions (friends feed, public feed, direct messages)
- Improve and optimize app performance
- Ensure security and prevent fraud

## Data Storage and Security

### Firebase Services

We use Google Firebase services for:
- **Authentication**: Secure sign-in with Apple
- **Cloud Storage**: Storing audio recordings and profile pictures
- **Firestore Database**: Storing user profiles, waffles metadata, and social connections

All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS and at rest using Firebase's security protocols.

### Data Retention

- **Audio Recordings**: Stored until you delete them or delete your account
- **Profile Pictures**: Stored until you change them or delete your account
- **Account Data**: Retained until you request account deletion

## Information Sharing

We DO NOT:
- Sell your personal information to third parties
- Share your data with advertisers
- Use your information for marketing purposes

We MAY share information:
- With other users as part of the app's social features (friends feed, public feed)
- When required by law or legal process
- To protect our rights and safety

## Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access** your personal data
- **Update** your profile information and recordings
- **Delete** your account and all associated data
- **Control** who sees your waffles (friends only vs. public)

### How to Delete Your Data

To delete your account and all associated data:
1. Open the app
2. Go to your Profile tab
3. Tap "Delete Account" (contact support if you need assistance)

All your audio recordings, profile pictures, and personal information will be permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days.

## Children's Privacy

Waffle Wednesday is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information.

## Permissions We Request

### Microphone Access
Required to record audio waffles. You can revoke this permission in your device settings at any time.

### Photo Library Access
Required to upload profile pictures. You can revoke this permission in your device settings at any time.

### Contacts Access (Optional)
Used to help you find friends who use Waffle Wednesday. We do not store your entire contacts list on our servers.

## Your California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):
- Right to know what personal information is collected
- Right to know whether your personal information is sold or disclosed
- Right to say no to the sale of personal information
- Right to access your personal information
- Right to equal service and price

We do not sell your personal information.

## International Users

Waffle Wednesday is operated in the United States. If you are located outside the U.S., please be aware that information we collect will be transferred to and processed in the United States.

## Data Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures including:
- Encryption of data in transit and at rest
- Secure authentication via Sign in with Apple
- Regular security audits
- Access controls and monitoring

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
